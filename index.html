Meeting Notes - Start: 3:03pm

F. Ridley: Once again, Congrats to Jon Rahm on winning the Masters. Great comback performance to claim the title!

Weather Discussion

  • Check the Trees! - cannot have patrons in danger of trees falling on them. While what happened was a rare occurrence, bad look on maintence - cannot happen again
  • Drainage systems - quite high tech already, need to ensure greens stay dry - had complaints of slow greens during second + third round. Reevalute fairway drainage to ensure no puddles
  • Check Forecast - as tournament approaches, tailor plan such that precautions are made specific to the inclement weather that may arrive - need to stay on top of this
  • Work scheduling around bad weather if present - try and get players out earlier if weather going to hit later in day = more holes in per day so players don't have to play 30 holes on Sunday

Pace of Play

  • Already Slow- Majors are already quite slow, length of rounds has already been well documented - is there anything we can really do to make them faster?
  • Average tournament round: 4hr 45min
  • Final 3 groups especially slow 2023 Masters - total round time over 5 hours 15min on average. Mainly complaints about second to last pairing. Overall slow day
  • Major tournaments are very important to players, especially those at top of leaderboard - should this not be as big a concern, even if some players play faster than others?
  • Currently nothing can be done about pace of play - there are limits on time waiting before hitting a shot, but limits are quite vaugue
  • May also be a one time thing - not going to constantly occur
  • Possible Solutions
  • Apply stroke penalty for too much time taken on a hole w/o previous penalty/drops - must be within reason, if hole takes 30min+ to complete(one idea)
  • Add more time between tee times on final (2?) rounds

  • Possible realignment of tee times, would have to expect a later end time
